# Release Notes — v0.X

Server-side / Apex section is owned by Marlowe — see her section in this file. UI section follows.

## UI changes since Phase 1 baseline

Phase 1 shipped a working but plain panel: a single flat list of engaged contacts, a basic "Add to Deal Team" modal, and a "View all" link that opened a grouped detail view. v0.X is what landed after the UX polish pass. The behavioral surface of the feature didn't change — the new affordances surface intelligence that the data layer was already producing.

### Vertical buying-motion timeline (new)

The [`engagementDetailModal`](../development/components/engagementDetailModal.md) gains a vertical timeline on the right side of the body. Each colored dot is one asset engagement, positioned vertically by date.

The scale is **piecewise** — the top half of the axis covers the last 6 weeks at weekly granularity (where momentum lives), the bottom half compresses 6 weeks → 1 year ago at monthly granularity, and anything older clamps at the bottom. See [`timeAgoToPercent`](../../force-app/main/default/lwc/engagementDetailModal/engagementDetailModal.js).

Dot color encodes persona: blue Executive, navy Finance, green Technical, orange Operational, gray Other. The chart caps at 100 dots, most-recent-first, so a high-volume Account still surfaces the freshest activity.

**Why it matters for sales:** the panel always told you who engaged. The timeline tells you _when_. CFO downloads clustering in the last week is a different signal than the same CFO last touching anything 8 months ago.

### Click-to-focus on the timeline (new)

Click a person row in Group-by-Person mode → the timeline filters to just that contact's touches and the header changes to `<Name>'s buying motion`. Collapse the row to clear the focus. See [`toggleRow`](../../force-app/main/default/lwc/engagementDetailModal/engagementDetailModal.js).

This makes the timeline a per-contact deep-dive without losing the all-up view. The implementation reuses the same expand-collapse handler that drives the per-asset breakdown — one click, both surfaces update.

### Stats strip (new)

Four tiles across the top of [`engagementDetailModal`](../development/components/engagementDetailModal.md):

- **Total Engaged** — `engagements.length`
- **Total Touches** — sum of touch counts
- **Top Topic** — the most-engaged Topic across the visible population, weighted by per-contact touch count
- **Buying-Committee Gap** — count of engaged contacts NOT on Deal Team

Top Topic truncates to 20 chars in the tile and surfaces the un-truncated value via `title` attribute (tooltip + screen-reader). See [`get stats`](../../force-app/main/default/lwc/engagementDetailModal/engagementDetailModal.js).

**Why it matters:** the panel rows tell a per-person story; the stats strip rolls up to the deal-level "are we covering the buying committee or not" question that a Sales Manager will ask first.

### Per-row dismiss `×` (new)

Every row in both [`engagementPanel`](../development/components/engagementPanel.md) and [`engagementDetailModal`](../development/components/engagementDetailModal.md) gains a small `×` button on the right.

Clicking it hides the contact from the user's view via [`EngagementController.dismissContact`](../../force-app/main/default/classes/engagement/EngagementController.cls). The dismissal is **per-user, per-scope, and auto-clearing** — the contact reappears as soon as a new `Engagement_Touch__c` arrives for them. A toast confirms (`<Name> hidden until a new touch arrives.`).

**Why it matters:** lets a rep say "I've seen this; not relevant unless something changes" without polluting other reps' views or losing the data. Reduces noise without permanent silencing.

### Hotlinks + Lightning hovercards (new)

Every contact name in the panel and the detail modal is now a real anchor (`<a href="/lightning/r/Contact/.../view">`). Three layers of progressive disclosure:

1. Click → navigate to the Contact record.
2. Hover briefly → multi-line `title` tooltip (touch count, topics, last-touch, OCR/ACR status, consultant flag) — built by [`buildSummaryTooltip`](../../force-app/main/default/lwc/engagementPanel/engagementPanel.js).
3. Hover ~1 second → Lightning's standard record hovercard auto-attaches because the href matches `/lightning/r/.../view`.

The detail modal also adds an **Open <RecordName> →** link at the top of the body — same three-layer treatment, but linking to the underlying Account or Opportunity record.

**Why it matters:** no more "click out, lose context" flow. The rep gets the full record preview without leaving the panel.

### Avatar color-coding for ACR (new)

Row avatars gain a subtle tint when the contact is reached via `AccountContactRelation`:

- Blue tint (`.av-consultant`) when `isConsultant` (e.g. Deloitte-attached)
- Gray tint (`.av-acr`) for any other external ACR (e.g. an independent advisor)
- Default for direct Account contacts

See [`avatarClassFor`](../../force-app/main/default/lwc/engagementPanel/engagementPanel.js). Combined with the `Consultant` / `ACR` badges in the action area, the rep can distinguish ecosystem influence from insider engagement at a glance.

### What didn't change

For the avoidance of doubt, behavior preserved from Phase 1:

- The two-section partition on the Opportunity panel (`Deal Team` / `Engaged — not on Deal Team`) is unchanged.
- The "+ Add to Deal Team" → role-picker → optional Already-Added-on-race flow is unchanged.
- The Group-by-Person / Group-by-Campaign toggle in the detail modal is unchanged.
- The Account vs Opportunity scoping behavior is unchanged — the same component, the same Apex methods, the same partition logic.

## Known UX limitations rolling into the next release

- **Top-Topic tile truncates silently at 20 chars.** Title attribute surfaces the full string but a longer label would deserve a wider tile or a 2-line wrap.
- **Timeline dot cap is silent.** No "+N more" affordance when an Account exceeds 100 dots.
- **`recordName` is not auto-resolved.** Parent has to pass it for the detail modal header; the LWC doesn't `@wire(getRecord)` to pull `Name`.
- **Primary OCR conflict isn't surfaced.** Ticking "Make primary" silently demotes the prior Primary per standard SF behavior. Flagged as Phase 1.5.
- **Hovercard isn't attached on `OpportunityContactRole` ids** (specifically in [`alreadyAddedModal`](../development/components/alreadyAddedModal.md)) — the modal mentions the OCR id but doesn't render an inline link to preview it.

Track these in the project backlog. None are blocking for v0.X.
